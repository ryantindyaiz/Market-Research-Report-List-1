<p><p>The BioPharmaceutical Tubing Market encompasses three primary types: plastics, metals, and silicone. Plastic tubing, often made from materials like PVC or polyolefin, is lightweight and offers chemical resistance, making it suitable for various applications. Metal tubing, typically stainless steel, provides durability and high pressure resistance, ideal for demanding environments. Silicone tubing is valued for its flexibility, biocompatibility, and ability to withstand extreme temperatures, making it essential for applications requiring sterility and flexibility in handling biological fluids.</p></p>
