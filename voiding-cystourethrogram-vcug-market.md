VCUG helps diagnose conditions such as vesicoureteral reflux, bladder abnormalities, urinary tract infections, and urethral obstructions.</p><p>The VCUG market is witnessing substantial growth, driven by an increasing prevalence of urinary tract disorders and a rising awareness of early diagnosis.
