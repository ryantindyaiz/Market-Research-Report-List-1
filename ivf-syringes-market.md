<p><ul><li>Intramuscular Injection</li><li>Subcutaneous Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IVF syringes market is categorized primarily into two types based on the injection methods: intramuscular and subcutaneous. Intramuscular injections involve delivering medication directly into the muscle tissue, offering rapid absorption and effectiveness, commonly used for hormone therapies in IVF. In contrast, subcutaneous injections are administered into the fatty tissue just beneath the skin, providing a less invasive option with ease of self-administration. Both methods are essential in fertility treatments, ensuring optimal medication delivery for successful outcomes.</p></p>
