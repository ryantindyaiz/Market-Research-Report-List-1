<p><p>The sintered metal filters market is divided into three primary porosity types: low, medium, and high. Low porosity filters (below 30%) offer excellent strength and durability, ideal for coarse filtration applications. Medium porosity filters (30-60%) provide a balanced combination of filtration efficiency and flow rate, suitable for diverse industrial uses. High porosity filters (above 60%) enable higher flow rates and are essential for fine filtration needs, often used in applications requiring minimal pressure drop and maximum particle retention.</p></p>
