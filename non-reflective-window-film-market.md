<p><p>The non-reflective window film market includes two primary types: clear and dyed films. Clear films offer a transparent surface that provides UV protection and heat reduction without altering the appearance of the glass. They maintain visibility while enhancing energy efficiency. Dyed films, on the other hand, feature a tinted layer that absorbs solar heat and glare, providing privacy and aesthetic appeal. Both types are popular for residential and commercial applications, catering to diverse needs for comfort and functionality.</p></p>
