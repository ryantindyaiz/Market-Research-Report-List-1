<p><strong>The Wafer Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tape for Grinding</li><li>Tape for Dicing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer tape market is divided into two main types: tape for grinding and tape for dicing. Tape for grinding is designed to securely hold semiconductor wafers during the grinding process, ensuring precision and efficiency while minimizing defects. In contrast, tape for dicing is used to attach wafers to substrates before they are cut into individual dies, providing stability and protecting the wafers from damage. Both types are essential for enhancing the performance and quality of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
