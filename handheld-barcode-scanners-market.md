<p><p>The handheld barcode scanners market encompasses various types based on connectivity and functionality. Bluetooth scanners offer wireless convenience, connecting to devices without physical cables. Keyboard wedge scanners interface directly with a keyboard port, allowing easy data transfer. Serial scanners communicate through serial ports, ensuring reliable connections for older systems. USB scanners connect directly to computers, offering fast data transfer. Wireless scanners provide greater mobility and flexibility, eliminating wires while maintaining a robust connection for efficient data capture and processing.</p></p>
