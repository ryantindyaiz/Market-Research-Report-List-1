<p><strong>The PVC-C Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Schedule 40</li><li>Schedule 80</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC-C pipes are categorized into different schedules based on their wall thickness and pressure ratings, primarily Schedule 40 and Schedule 80. Schedule 40 pipes are lighter and suitable for low to moderate pressure applications, often used in plumbing and irrigation systems. In contrast, Schedule 80 pipes have thicker walls, enabling them to withstand higher pressures and demanding industrial applications. Both types are valued for their corrosion resistance, durability, and ease of installation, making them essential in various sectors.</p></p>
