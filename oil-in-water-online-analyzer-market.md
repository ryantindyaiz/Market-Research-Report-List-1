<p><p>The Oil in Water Online Analyzer market primarily features two key technologies: Infrared Spectrophotometry and UV Fluorescence Detection Method. Infrared Spectrophotometry measures the absorption of infrared light by oil molecules in water, providing accurate concentration readings. In contrast, UV Fluorescence Detection utilizes ultraviolet light to excite oil molecules, causing them to emit fluorescence, which indicates oil presence and concentration. Both methods are essential for monitoring water quality in various industries, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and safeguarding ecological health.</p></p>
