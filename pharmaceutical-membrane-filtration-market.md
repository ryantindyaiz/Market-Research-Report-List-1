MCE membrane filters are known for their excellent particle retention and low extractables. Coated cellulose acetate filters offer enhanced hydrophobic properties, making them suitable for aqueous solutions. Nylon membrane filters provide superior chemical compatibility and durability. PVDF filters are recognized for their high thermal stability and low protein binding. Other filters may include PTFE and PES membranes, catering to specific filtration needs in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
