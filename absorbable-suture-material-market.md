<p><ul><li>Synthetic Material</li><li>Natural Material</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absorbable suture material market is segmented into synthetic and natural materials. Synthetic absorbable sutures, made from materials like polyglycolic acid and polylactic acid, offer consistent tensile strength and predictable absorption rates. They are often favored for their versatility in various surgical procedures. Natural absorbable sutures, derived from biological sources like gut or catgut, provide biocompatibility but may have variable absorption rates. Both types are essential for promoting healing while minimizing the need for suture removal.</p></p>
