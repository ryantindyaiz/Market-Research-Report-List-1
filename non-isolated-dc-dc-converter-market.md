<p><ul><li>Step-down</li><li>Step-up</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-isolated DC-DC converters are classified primarily into step-down (buck), step-up (boost), and other types. Step-down converters reduce input voltage to a lower output voltage, making them ideal for applications that require lower voltage levels. Step-up converters increase input voltage to a higher output voltage, suitable for powering devices needing greater voltage. Other types may include buck-boost converters, which can either step up or step down voltage, and specific configurations catering to unique application requirements, enhancing flexibility in power management.</p></p>
