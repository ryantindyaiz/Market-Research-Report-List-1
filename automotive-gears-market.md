<p><p>The automotive gears market includes several types, each serving distinct functions. Spur gears are used for simple, straight-line power transmission. Helical gears provide smoother operation and are ideal for high-speed applications. Bevel gears facilitate changes in direction and are often used in differentials. Worm gears offer high torque reduction in compact spaces. Gear racks convert rotational motion into linear motion, useful in steering systems. Other gear types include planetary gears and hypoid gears, catering to specialized automotive needs.</p></p>
