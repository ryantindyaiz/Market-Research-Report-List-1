<p><p>The Vehicle Torque Analyzer market is categorized into two main types: Static Torque Analyzers and Dynamic Torque Analyzers. Static Torque Analyzers measure torque when a vehicle is stationary, providing insights into components like fasteners and joints under no movement, essential for quality control and safety testing. Conversely, Dynamic Torque Analyzers assess torque during vehicle operation, capturing real-time performance data that reflects the effects of forces in motion, critical for performance tuning and diagnosing operational issues.</p></p>
