<p><p>Lasers in the additive manufacturing market play a crucial role in processes like Stereolithography (SLA), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). SLA uses lasers to cure liquid resin into solid parts, enabling high precision and detail. SLS employs lasers to fuse powdered materials, creating durable structures without the need for support. SLM, on the other hand, melts metallic powders layer by layer, achieving fully dense components. These technologies cater to diverse applications in aerospace, automotive, and healthcare sectors.</p></p>
