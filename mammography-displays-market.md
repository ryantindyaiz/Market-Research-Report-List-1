<p><p>The mammography displays market consists primarily of two types: LED-backlit and LCD-backlit displays. In contrast, LCD-backlit monitors utilize fluorescent lighting and typically offer lower luminance and color performance. As healthcare providers seek improved diagnostic capabilities, LED-backlit displays are gaining preference due to their clarity and enhanced visualization of subtle breast tissue details.</p></p>
