<p><p>The building facade cleaning robot market comprises two main types: contact and non-contact cleaning robots. Contact cleaning robots physically engage with surfaces using brushes or pads to remove dirt and grime. They are effective for thorough cleaning but may require careful surface compatibility. Non-contact cleaning robots, on the other hand, utilize advanced technologies such as high-pressure water jets or ultrasonic waves, minimizing the risk of surface damage. Both types cater to diverse building materials and differing cleaning requirements in urban environments.</p></p>
