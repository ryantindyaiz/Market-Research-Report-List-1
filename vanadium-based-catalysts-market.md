<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vanadium-Based Catalysts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>γ-alumina Vanadium-Based Catalysts</li><li>Pseudo-boehmite Vanadium-Based Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vanadium-based catalysts are classified primarily into two types: γ-alumina vanadium-based catalysts and pseudo-boehmite vanadium-based catalysts. γ-alumina vanadium-based catalysts utilize γ-alumina as a support, known for its high surface area and thermal stability, enhancing catalytic activity in various chemical processes. Pseudo-boehmite vanadium-based catalysts, on the other hand, are derived from pseudo-boehmite, offering improved dispersion of vanadium and tailored properties for specific applications. Both types play crucial roles in industrial processes like petroleum refining and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
