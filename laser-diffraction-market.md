<p><p>Laser diffraction is a powerful analytical technique used to measure the size distribution of particles and droplets based on the diffraction of laser light. When a laser beam passes through a sample, the particles scatter the light at various angles, allowing for precise measurement of their sizes. This technique is widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and materials science for quality control and research purposes.</p><p>The Laser Diffraction Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for particle size analysis in various applications. Industries are recognizing the advantages of laser diffraction, including high accuracy, rapid results, and the ability to analyze a wide size range. The trend toward automation and integration of laser diffraction systems with advanced data analysis software is gaining traction, enhancing efficiency and reproducibility in measurements.</p><p>Moreover, the rise in research and development activities across sectors is propelling market expansion.
