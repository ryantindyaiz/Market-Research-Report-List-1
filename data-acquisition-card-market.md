<p><strong>The Data Acquisition Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PXI Card</li><li>CPCI Card</li><li>PCI Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data acquisition card market is segmented into PXI (PCI eXtensions for Instrumentation), CPCI (Compact PCI), and PCI (Peripheral Component Interconnect) cards. PXI cards are designed for automated test systems and high-speed data processing, offering modularity and scalability. CPCI cards cater to industrial applications with robust, extended temperature ranges and reliability. PCI cards are traditional interfaces used for various general-purpose applications, providing reliable data transfer but with limited expansion compared to PXI and CPCI options.</p></p>
