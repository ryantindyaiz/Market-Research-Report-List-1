<p><ul><li>Mesophilic Type</li><li>Thermophilic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cheese starter culture market is categorized into mesophilic and thermophilic types. Mesophilic cultures thrive at moderate temperatures and are used in soft cheeses like mozzarella and cream cheese, promoting desirable flavors and textures. In contrast, thermophilic cultures operate at higher temperatures, suitable for aged cheeses such as Parmesan and Swiss. These cultures play a crucial role in fermentation, influencing the cheese's taste, aroma, and consistency, making them essential for artisanal and mass-produced cheese varieties alike.</p></p>
