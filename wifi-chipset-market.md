<p><p>The Wi-Fi chipset market is categorized into several standards, including 802.11n, 802.11ac, and 802.11ad, each offering distinct capabilities. The 802.11n standard supports increased speeds and range by utilizing MIMO technology. 802.11ac, known for its higher throughput, operates on the 5 GHz band and supports multiple connections. 802.11ad offers ultra-fast wireless communication using the 60 GHz band, ideal for high-bandwidth applications. The "Others" segment includes emerging technologies and standards that cater to diverse connectivity needs in various devices.</p></p>
