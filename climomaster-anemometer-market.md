<p><ul><li>Constant Temperature</li><li>Constant Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Climomaster Anemometer market features two primary types: Constant Temperature and Constant Current. Constant Temperature anemometers maintain a steady temperature to measure airflow by detecting variations in heat loss, which correlates with wind speed. In contrast, Constant Current anemometers operate by maintaining a constant electrical current through a sensing element, measuring changes in the element's resistance to determine airflow. Both methods serve specific application needs, ensuring accurate wind measurement across diverse environments and industries.</p></p>
