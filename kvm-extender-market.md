<p><ul><li>Extender Pair</li><li>Receiver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The KVM (Keyboard, Video, Mouse) extender market is primarily divided into two categories: extender pairs and receiver markets. Extender pairs consist of a transmitter and receiver that work together to transmit signals over long distances, allowing remote access to computer systems. On the other hand, the receiver market focuses on standalone devices that receive signals from various sources, enhancing flexibility in configurations. Both serve essential functions in improving workstation efficiency and distance access in professional environments.</p></p>
