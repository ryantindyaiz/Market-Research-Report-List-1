<p><p>The three-dimensional five-axis laser cutting equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: fiber lasers and carbon dioxide (CO2) lasers. Fiber lasers utilize solid-state technology, offering high efficiency, excellent beam quality, and versatility for cutting various materials, particularly metals. In contrast, CO2 lasers are predominantly used for cutting non-metal materials like wood, plastics, and textiles, providing high power and a longer wavelength. Both types cater to different applications and industries, contributing significantly to advancements in precision cutting technologies.</p></p>
