<p><p>The smartphone 3D sensing technology market comprises several types, each leveraging unique principles for depth perception. Stereoscopic vision uses dual cameras to create a 3D image by mimicking human vision. Structured light projects patterns to capture depth data accurately. Time of Flight (ToF) measures distance by calculating the time light takes to return from the subject. Ultrasound employs sound waves to gauge depth, while "Others" includes emerging technologies that utilize diverse methods for enhanced 3D sensing capabilities in smartphones.</p></p>
