<p><ul><li>Complete Electrification</li><li>Partial Electrification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transportation electrification encompasses two primary market types: complete electrification and partial electrification. Complete electrification involves the full transition from conventional fossil fuel-powered vehicles to electric vehicles (EVs), including personal cars, buses, and freight systems. This market aims for sustainability and reduced emissions. Partial electrification, on the other hand, integrates electric and hybrid technologies within traditional systems, such as plug-in hybrids and electric rail systems, allowing for a gradual shift while balancing existing infrastructure and technology.</p></p>
