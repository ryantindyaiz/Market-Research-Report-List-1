<p><p>The hydrogenated copolymer resin market is categorized into three primary types: C5-C9, C9-DCPD, and DCPD-C5. C5-C9 resins are derived from a mix of cyclopentenes and aliphatic compounds, offering versatility in adhesives and coatings. C9-DCPD resins, combining C9 and dicyclopentadiene, are known for their enhanced thermal stability and strength, making them suitable for industrial applications. DCPD-C5 resins feature a combination of dicyclopentadiene and C5 components, focusing on performance in high-end formulations like rubber adhesives and sealants.</p></p>
